The Ripple format exists to make an invisible cost visible. Nobody litters thinking
"I will flood my own street tonight" — the gap between the small act and the large
consequence is the whole joke. We end on the offender paying for it himself, because
a stranger suffering reads as a lecture; the offender suffering reads as comedy.
