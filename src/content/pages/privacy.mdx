Short version: no accounts, no tracking, no cookies, nothing to sell. This page
explains the little that does happen.

## What stays in your browser

One setting, your light or dark theme choice, is saved in your browser's local
storage so the site remembers it next time. It never leaves your device and we
cannot read it.

## What we never do

We do not sell data, run ad networks, or hand anything to third parties for
marketing. There is nothing here to harvest.

## Contact

Questions about privacy? Open an issue on
[GitHub](https://github.com/Gopal-Khadka/civic-sense) and we will answer in the
open.
