title: Six ways to sting
description: The same lesson, told through a handful of repeatable shapes. Each one is built to make you wince — at yourself.
order: 1
---

Every strip is written from a recurring **format** — a reliable comedic mechanic we
can fill with any civic sin. Formats keep production fast (writing a strip is a few
lines of dialogue, not hours of drawing) and keep the tone consistent across the site.

The rule across all of them: **universal mission, specific punchlines.** We never draw
"rude people in general" — we draw the one person reclining their seat into your knees.
