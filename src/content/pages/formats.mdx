title: Six ways to sting
description: The same lesson, told through a handful of repeatable shapes.
order: 1
---

Every strip runs on a format: a reliable comic setup we can pour any civic sin
into. Formats keep the writing fast and the tone steady from one strip to the next.

One rule holds across all six. Universal target, specific punchline. We never
draw "rude people in general." We draw the one person reclining their seat
straight into your knees.
